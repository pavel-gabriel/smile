# Decisions

> Record significant architectural, technical, and process decisions here.
> Small tactical choices don't belong here — only decisions that have lasting impact on the project.
>
> Use the template below for each entry. Number decisions sequentially.

---

## Template

```
### DEC-XXX — Title

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded
**Context:** Why did this come up? What options were considered?
**Decision:** What was decided?
**Rationale:** Why this option over the alternatives?
**Consequences:** What changes? What tradeoffs or debt does this introduce?
**Supersedes:** DEC-XXX (if applicable)
```
